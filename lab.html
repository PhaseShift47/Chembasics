Lab Basics & Safety

Essential knowledge before entering the chemistry lab.

⚠️ Important Lab Safety Rules

  • Always wear ANSI-approved safety goggles and a lab coat.
  • Never eat, drink, or chew gum in the laboratory.
  • Know the locations of the fire extinguisher, eye wash, and safety shower.
  • Always add acid to water (never water to acid) to prevent splashing and uncontrolled boiling.
  • Report all accidents, spills, and broken glassware to the instructor immediately.
